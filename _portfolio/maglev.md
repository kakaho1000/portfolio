### Goal 
Create a magnetic levitator as the final project of the 1-month long MECH 220 technical skills practicum.

### Process
The entire enclosure was manufactured in the machine shop, using mills, lathes, brake presses, power tools, and drills. I also soldered all of the components onto the PCB, learning about diodes, transistors, fuses and op-amps. Oscilloscopes were used to troubleshoot.
